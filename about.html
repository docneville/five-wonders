About

What is Five Wonders?

Five Wonders is an invite-only app for sharing the places you actually love — the coffee shops, bakeries, parks, bookstores, and weird little spots you’d recommend to a close friend, not an algorithm.

How it works

  • Add – Use the Add page to search for a place or drop a pin, tweak the name, and write why it matters to you.
  • View – Open the View map to explore friends’ recommendations, with notes and context.
  • Edit – Use the Edit page to update or remove places you’ve added.

Who can use it?

Five Wonders is currently an invite-only beta. Each person has a private invite code that links their saved places to their profile. This lets the map show who saved what, and eventually enables filtering by friends.

Why does it exist?

Most review platforms optimize for volume and engagement. Five Wonders is the opposite: a small, trusted layer of recommendations on top of a map — powered by people you actually know.
