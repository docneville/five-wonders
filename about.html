About

What is Five Wonders?

Five Wonders is a tiny, invite-only map for sharing the places you actually love — the coffee shops, bakeries, parks, bookstores, and weird little spots you’d send to a close friend, not an algorithm.

How it works

  • Add – Use the Add page to search a place on the map, tweak the name, and add your own note about why it’s great.
  • View – Open the View map to see everyone’s saved places, with markers you can click for notes and who saved it.
  • Edit – Use the Edit page to update or remove places you’ve added.

Who can use it?

Five Wonders is currently an invite-only beta. Each person gets a private invite code that ties their saved places to their profile. That lets the map show who saved what, and eventually makes it possible to filter by friends.

Why does it exist?

Most review sites optimize for volume. Five Wonders is the opposite: a small, trusted layer on top of the map, powered by people you actually know.
