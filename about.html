About Five Wonders

  • What is Five Wonders?

    Five Wonders is an invite-only app for sharing the places you actually love — the coffee shops, bakeries, parks, bookstores, and weird little spots you'd recommend to a close friend, not an algorithm.

  • How it works

    Add – Use the Add page to search a place on the map or just drop a pin, tweak the name, and add your own note about why it's great.

    View – Open the View map to see your friends' saved places, with markers you can click for notes and more info.

    Edit – Use the Edit page to update or remove places you've added.

  • Who can use it?

    Right now Five Wonders is an invite-only beta. Each person gets a private invite code that ties their saved places to their profile. That lets the View map show who saved what, and eventually makes it possible to filter by friends.

  • Why does it exist?

    Most review platforms optimize for volume and engagement (TripAdvisor, Yelp, Google). Five Wonders is the opposite: trusted recommendations layered on top of a map, powered by people you actually know.
