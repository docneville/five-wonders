About

What is Five Wonders?

Five Wonders is an invite-only app for sharing the places you actually love — the coffee shops, bakeries, parks, bookstores, and weird little spots you’d recommend to a close friend, not an algorithm.

Why it exists

Most review sites optimize for volume. Five Wonders is the opposite: a small, trusted layer of recommendations on top of a map — powered by people you actually know.

How it works

  • Add – Use Add to search or drop a pin, tweak the name, and write why it’s great.
  • View – Open View to explore friends’ recommendations with notes and context.
  • Edit – Use Edit to update or remove places you’ve added.

Who can use it?

Right now Five Wonders is an invite-only beta. Each person gets a private invite code that links their saved places to their profile. This lets the map show who saved what, and eventually enables filtering by friends.
